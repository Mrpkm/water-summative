Water: The Drivers — Problems — Solutions

Water cycle is the path that our water takes as it moves around the planet. Heat from sunlight causes the water to evaporate. Water from plants and trees can also evaporate this is called transpiration. Over time, water vapor piles up in the atmosphere, resulting in clouds.
