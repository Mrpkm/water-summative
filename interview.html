Mr. Andy Interview

A first-hand interview about water use, cost and conservation. The interviewee’s answers are shown exactly as recorded.

❓The questions & answers

1. Where are you from (Country and city)?

MANCHESTER, UK.

2. Do you know about the water cycle? Do you know its importance?

YES

3. Did you have easy access to water in your home country?

YES

4. How do you use water daily at home, at work and in your free time?

YES

5. Do you consider that you use water responsibly and sustainably in your daily life? Why / why not?

YES. I DO THE ACTIONS THAT I WAS TAUGHT FOR WATER CONSERVATION, BUT I MAY NEED TO LEARN NEW WAYS.

6. Do you think you pay a lot for this service? Should it be free? Why or why not?

I DO NOT PAY MUCH. WE SHOULD PAY PRIVATELY OR BY THE TAX SYSTEM. THIS SHOULD BE ON A SLIDING SCALE OF AFFORDABILITY.

7. What is the main or most important source of water in your country?

RIVERS AND AQUIFERS

8. What is the biggest water problem or crisis currently affecting your country or city of origin?

WATER SHORTAGES DUE TO 68MILLION PEOPLES HIGH CONSUMPTION, EVEN WITH ALL THE RAIN!

9. How does this problem affect the local climate, ecosystems, or the water distribution in that region?

LESS WATER FOR HUMAN CONSUMPTION

10. How does this problem (Scarcity, pollution, high cost, waste) affect the daily lives, health, and economy of the people who live there?

AT THE MOMENT ITS OK DUE TO CONSTANT RAINFALL, BUT THE GENERIC HEALTH IMPACTS AND ECONOMY WITH FACTORIES NOT GETTING CLEAN WATER TO COOL THE MACHINES DOWN IN THE FACTORS CAN RIPPLE THE HDI’S

11. Do you think everyone in your country has equal access to clean drinking water, or do some communities suffer more?

NO THEY DO NOT

12. What ideas/measures have you or your community taken to help reduce/mitigate or solve this water issue?

HOSE PIPE BANS, WATER METER IN HOMES, EDUCATION

13. In your role as a teacher, how do you motivate students/ encourage them to be more aware of water conservation?

WE DONT COVER IT, BUT I ADHERE TO BEST APPROACHES ADVERTISEMENT WHEN I SEE IT.
